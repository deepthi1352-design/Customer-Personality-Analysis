README – Customer Personality Analysis Using Power BI

Project Type: Data Analytics / Business Intelligence Project

Tool Used: Microsoft Power BI

Dataset: Customer Personality Analysis (Marketing Campaign Dataset)

Domain: Retail / Marketing Analytics

1. Project Overview

This project analyzes customer demographic details, purchasing behavior, and marketing campaign responses using Power BI. The objective is to convert raw customer data into interactive dashboards that help understand customer segments, spending patterns, and campaign effectiveness.

2. Problem Statement

Businesses often collect large amounts of customer data but struggle to identify valuable insights. This project aims to analyze customer characteristics and purchasing behavior to support better marketing decisions and customer targeting strategies.

3. Objectives

Analyze customer demographics such as education and marital status.

Study customer purchasing behavior across different product categories.

Compare online, catalog, and store purchases.

Evaluate the performance of marketing campaigns.

Create interactive dashboards for business decision-making.

4. Dataset Description

The Customer Personality Analysis dataset contains customer demographic details, purchasing behavior, and marketing campaign responses. It includes 2,240 customer records and 29 attributes, covering customer information, product spending, purchase channels, and campaign performance. This dataset is used to analyze customer behavior, identify purchasing patterns, and evaluate the effectiveness of marketing campaigns through interactive Power BI dashboards.

5. Data Preprocessing

Checked for missing values in the Income column.

Verified data types for numeric and categorical fields.

Removed unnecessary spaces and formatting issues.

Created required measures in Power BI.

Measures Created:

Total Purchases = NumWebPurchases + NumCatalogPurchases + NumStorePurchases

Total Campaign Accepted = AcceptedCmp1 + AcceptedCmp2 + AcceptedCmp3 + AcceptedCmp4 + AcceptedCmp5


6. Dashboard Design


This project consists of three interactive dashboards created using different Power BI visualizations.

Dashboard 1 – Customer Profile Analysis - Customer Profile: Analyzes customer education, marital status, and income distribution. 

Donut Chart: Customer distribution by Education.

Treemap: Customer distribution by Education.

Bar Chart: Customer distribution by Marital Status.

Column Chart: Income by Education.


Dashboard 2 – Customer Purchase Analysis - Examines product spending, website visits, and customer purchasing behavior.


Bar Chart: Product spending across different categories.

Scatter Chart: Website visits and product spending analysis.

Column Chart: Comparison of purchase channels.


Area Chart: Distribution of web purchases.


Dashboard 3 – Marketing Campaign Analysis - Evaluates campaign performance, customer responses, and key marketing insights using charts and KPI cards.


Bar Chart: Comparison of marketing campaign acceptance.

Pie Chart: Customer response distribution.

Area Chart: Income analysis based on customer response and education.

KPI Cards: Total values of campaign acceptance for quick performance analysis.


These dashboards provide a clear visualization of customer demographics, purchasing behavior, and marketing campaign performance, making the data easy to understand and analyze.

7. Key Insights

Graduates form the largest customer segment.

Wine products contribute the highest customer spending.

Store purchases are higher than web purchases.

Customers with frequent website visits tend to make more online purchases.

Campaign 5 shows the highest acceptance among all campaigns.

8. Conclusion

The project successfully transformed raw customer data into meaningful business insights using Power BI. The dashboards provide a clear understanding of customer demographics, purchasing behavior, and marketing campaign performance. These insights can help organizations improve customer targeting, optimize marketing strategies, and increase business revenue.
